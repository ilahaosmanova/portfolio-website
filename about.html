About Me

You can learn more about my academic background, technical skills, and personal interests. This page highlights my journey, experiences, and the activities that shape my growth.

Academic Background

I am a first-year Computer Science student at ADA University where I was admitted in 2025. I started my General Education studies in Spring 2026. My courses cover a wide range of topics, including technical subjects in my major as well as global perspectives, writing and literature. Among my courses, Principles of Information Systems (SITE-1101) has been the most impactful so far. It helped me build a strong foundation in CS and improved my ability to work in a team. Throughout the course, we completed three group projects that were challenging but more enjoyable than any other course that I am currently taking. These projects pushed me to explore things beyond the classroom , including skills I never expected to improve, such as video editing. In the coming semesters, I will be taking more advanced courses in my major. I am particularly interested in cybersecurity and plan to focus my studies in that direction. I am looking forward to the challenge and excited to see how much further I can grow.

Activities and Hobbies

Outside of my studies, I am interested in volunteering, participating in intellectual games, and hackathons, which I find both enjoyable and mentally stimulating. These activities allow me to challenge myself, think critically, and collaborate with others in dynamic environments. They also help me maintain a healthy balance between my studies and personal life, allowing me to stay focused and productive. I find that taking time for these activities improves both my mental clarity and overall well-being.
