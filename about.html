Academic Background

I am a first-year Computer Science student at ADA University, where I was admitted in 2025. I started my General Education studies in the Spring 2026 semester. Currently, I am taking a variety of courses to improve not only my technical skills in my major, but also my knowledge in areas such as writing, politics, and literature. My Principles of Information Systems (SITE-1101) course has helped me build a strong foundation in my field and significantly improved my teamwork skills. During this course, we completed three group projects, which were sometimes challenging but highly engaging compared to other courses. In the upcoming semesters, I will take more advanced major courses, which I expect will be both challenging and interesting in a good way.
